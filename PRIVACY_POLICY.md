# Privacy Policy for StoryTots

**Effective date:** [INSERT DATE — e.g., June 9, 2026]

This Privacy Policy explains how **StoryTots** (the "App"), provided by
**RRNewTech** ("we", "us", or "our"), handles information. Please read it
carefully. By installing or using the App, you (the parent or legal guardian)
agree to the practices described here. If you do not agree, please do not use
the App.

StoryTots is a children's reading application intended to be set up and used
**under the supervision of a parent or guardian**.

---

## 1. Summary (the short version)

- **StoryTots works fully offline.** We do **not** operate servers and we do
  **not** collect, transmit, or upload your information to us or to any third
  party for our own use.
- **All information stays on your device.** Account details, your child's
  profile, and reading activity are stored locally in the App's private storage.
- **We do not sell or share personal data**, we do **not** display advertising,
  and we do **not** use analytics or third-party tracking SDKs.
- The App requests **microphone** access so your child can read aloud and
  receive feedback. Speech recognition is performed by your device's built-in
  speech service (see Section 4).
- You can delete all stored information at any time by clearing the App's data
  or uninstalling the App.

---

## 2. Information stored on your device

When you create an account and use the App, the following information is created
and stored **locally on your device only**:

**Account information**
- Email address (used as your local login identifier)
- Password (stored only as a salted cryptographic hash — never in plain text)

**Child profile information**
- Child's first name and last name
- Birth date
- Selected interests/topics, learning goal, and avatar

**Activity information**
- Reading progress (current position in a story)
- Reading time/minutes and language practiced
- Favorite stories, reading history, and completed stories

We do not have access to any of this information. It does not leave your device
except when **you** choose to share it (see Section 5, "Progress reports").

---

## 3. How information is used

Information stored on the device is used solely to provide the App's features,
including to:

- Let your child sign in and resume where they left off
- Personalize story recommendations based on selected interests
- Track reading progress, streaks, completed books, and time spent
- Display a parent-facing progress summary

Because the App is offline, this processing happens entirely on your device.

---

## 4. Microphone and speech recognition

StoryTots includes a read-aloud feature. To support it, the App requests
permission to use your device's **microphone**.

- Audio is captured only while a reading session is active and your child is
  using the read-aloud feature.
- Speech-to-text recognition is performed by your **device's operating system
  speech service** (for example, the Google speech service on Android or the
  Apple Speech framework on iOS). Depending on your device, settings, and
  operating system version, that service may process audio **on-device** or
  **in the cloud** under the policies of the operating system vendor.
- The App itself does **not** record, save, or transmit audio files, and does
  not retain recordings after a reading session.

Text-to-speech (the App reading text aloud) is likewise handled by your device's
built-in text-to-speech engine.

For more information about how your device handles speech, please review the
privacy policy of your device manufacturer / operating system provider
(e.g., Google or Apple).

## 5. Information sharing and disclosure

- **We do not sell personal information.**
- **We do not share personal information** with third parties for their own
  purposes.
- **No advertising or analytics.** The App contains no third-party advertising
  networks, analytics, or tracking technologies.

**Progress reports.** The App can generate a PDF progress report from the data
stored on your device. This report is created locally. It is shared **only if
you choose to share it** using your device's standard share feature (for
example, by email or a messaging app). When you do so, the content is handled by
the app or service you select, under that service's own privacy policy. We do
not receive a copy.

**Legal disclosures.** Because we do not collect or hold your data, we are not
able to disclose it in response to legal requests.

## 6. Children's privacy

StoryTots is designed for children and is intended to be configured and managed
by a parent or guardian.

- Child profile information is entered and controlled by the parent/guardian.
- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children, and we do not
  transmit children's personal information off the device for our use.
- All child-related data remains on the device under the control of the
  account holder.

We aim to align with applicable children's privacy laws and standards, including
the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), the EU General Data
Protection Regulation (GDPR), including provisions concerning children, and the
Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012. Because the App stores data only on your
device and we do not collect it, much of the data handling is within your direct
control as the parent or guardian.

If you believe a child has provided information in a way you did not intend, you
can remove it at any time by clearing the App's data or uninstalling the App.

## 8. Data retention and deletion

- Information is retained on your device until you delete it.
- To delete all stored information, you can:
  - **Clear the App's data** in your device settings (Settings → Apps →
    StoryTots → Storage → Clear data), or
  - **Uninstall the App**, which removes its local database and stored files.
- You may also remove individual items within the App (for example, removing a
  favorite).

Deleting the App's data is permanent and cannot be undone by us, because we do
not keep a copy.

---

## 9. Data security

- Your password is stored only as a **salted hash**, not as readable text.
- Data is stored in the App's **private storage area**, which is not accessible
  to other apps on a normal (non-modified) device.
- No security method is 100% secure. Because data is stored on your device, you
  should also protect the device itself with a screen lock and keep your
  operating system up to date.
