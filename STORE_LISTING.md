## Full description (Play limit: 4000 characters)

StoryTots — Read-Along Stories for Kids (English & Filipino)

StoryTots is a gentle, ad-free reading app that helps young children fall in love with reading. Kids read along to beautifully told stories in both English and Filipino (Tagalog), practice reading aloud, and build confidence one sentence at a time — all on a calm, colorful, kid-friendly screen.

Best of all, StoryTots works completely offline. No internet needed, no ads, and no tracking. Everything stays on your device.

WHY FAMILIES LOVE STORYTOTS
• Bilingual stories — switch between English and Filipino for every tale
• Read-along highlighting that follows the words like karaoke
• Read-aloud practice — your child reads and gets friendly feedback
• Listen mode — the app can read stories out loud
• Classic Filipino folk tales and timeless fables
• Personalized picks based on your child's chosen interests
• Cute avatars and a warm, distraction-free design
• Soothing background music and sound effects (fully adjustable)

GREAT FOR EARLY LEARNERS
StoryTots is designed for toddlers and early readers. Stories are short, the screens are simple, and the read-along and read-aloud features support letter sounds, vocabulary, and reading comprehension in a playful way.

FOR PARENTS
• Track reading minutes, streaks, completed books, and progress
• See English vs. Filipino practice at a glance
• Generate and share a PDF progress report whenever you like
• Set up a profile with your child's interests, goal, and avatar
• Manage sound and music settings

A LIBRARY OF STORIES
Enjoy beloved Filipino legends and classic fables, including:
• The Monkey and the Turtle
• Legend of the Banana (Alamat ng Saging)
• Legend of the Sampaguita
• The Legend of the Bitter Gourd
• The Legend of the Rainbow
• The Lion and the Mouse
• The Ant and the Grasshopper
• Why the Ocean Is Salty
• Why the Sky Is High
• The Carabao and the Shell
• Stories of Juan Tamad
• Legend of the Pineapple (Alamat ng Pinya)
…each available in English and Filipino.

PRIVACY YOU CAN TRUST
StoryTots is built to be safe for children:
• Works 100% offline — no internet connection required
• No ads and no third-party advertising
• No analytics or tracking
• Your child's information never leaves the device
You stay in control: clear the app's data anytime to remove everything.
